Who are we?

We are a team passionate about literature and literacy. ItsBook was founded with the aim to make books more accessible to everyone around the world. We believe that literacy is key to personal development and intellectual growth, and we are committed to supporting this mission by providing a platform that makes it easy to read books, share knowledge, and support independent authors.
